About

I turn ideas into shipped code. design systems. real products. things people use. shipped code, design systems, real products, and things people actually use.

I work at the seam between design and engineering, where good ideas either become real products or quietly fall apart.

My background spans digital strategy, user experience, and visual design, with deep, repeated experience building and scaling design systems that keep large organizations coherent. I don’t hand off at the mockup. I vibe the front-end code that turns a system into something people actually use.

That end-to-end fluency means fewer translation losses between teams, faster paths from concept to production, and design decisions that hold up once they meet the realities of the browser. I think in systems, communicate in plain language, and ship.
